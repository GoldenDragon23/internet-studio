About Tony Stark

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a billionaire inventor and philanthropist known for his brilliance, innovation, and leadership within Stark Industries and the Avengers. He has dedicated his life to using technology for the greater good of humanity.

With unmatched intelligence and a passion for progress, Tony would make a strong and visionary leader who values innovation, equality, and global peace.

Running Mate: Pepper Potts

Pepper Potts is a compassionate and strategic leader, known for her sharp decision-making and empathy. Her balance of intellect and emotional intelligence makes her the perfect partner for Tony Stark’s presidency.

Campaign Platform

Tony’s platform focuses on sustainable innovation, clean energy, and technology-driven education. His goal is to build a future where every individual has access to opportunity and advancement powered by science and compassion.
